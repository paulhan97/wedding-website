What options are available for bedding?

➕︎

Standard rooms enter you into a random lottery that results in one of the following bedding arrangements:

(1) King Bed

(2) 2-Queen Beds

The only way to guarantee bedding preference is to pay an addition $100 per room per night. However, if you have a strong preference for bedding but do not want to pay the additional charge, please reach out to either Kimia or Paul. We will reach out to the Mauna Lani team on your behalf. There is still no guarantee, but they may see your request sooner.

If you would like to have 3 adults in one room, please include kimiasadeghi98@gmail.com or paul.seungoh.han@gmail.com in your email to mnl.groups@aubergeresorts.com. We will help you get the extra person fee waived.

King Bed Rooms can only accomodate 3 individuals at most (with a rollaway bed); whereas, 2-Queen Bed Rooms can accomodate 2 adults with 2 children/teenagers. However, 2-Queen Bed Rooms cannot accomodate 4 adults without incurring an additional person charge of $100 per room per night
